Where It All Began

Spoon was born in a tiny home kitchen where our founder, a certified nutritionist, realized how hard it was to stick to a healthy lifestyle while balancing work, family, and time. With a passion for clean eating and a knack for meal prepping, she began crafting personalized meals for friends and family — and the demand quickly grew.

Our Mission

We believe that healthy eating should be easy, delicious, and sustainable. Our mission is simple: to help people achieve their goals through food that fits their body, lifestyle, and taste. Whether you're a busy professional, a parent, or an athlete — we’ve got your plate covered.

What Makes Us Different

  • Nutritionist-Curated Plans: Every menu is created with the help of certified experts to match your goals.
  • Locally Sourced Ingredients: We partner with farms and markets to ensure every bite is fresh and seasonal.
  • No Guilt, Just Flavor: We never sacrifice taste. Our chefs bring balance to every bowl and plate.

Today and Tomorrow

Today, Spoon delivers to hundreds of homes every week. But we’re just getting started. We’re investing in sustainability, new recipe technology, and expanding our reach — one spoon at a time.
